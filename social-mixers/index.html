Social Mixers

To emulate the random encounters and interactions which make up the true spice of in person conferences, we offer two sessions of social mixers. One from 12:00 to 12:45 UTC on Wednesday 1st of July and one from 16:45 to 17:30 UTC on Thursday 2nd of July.

To participate you need to register on this form: here.

Each session will consist in the creation of small random groups of registered people with a mix of senior and junior people in each group. You will be invited at the beginning of this event in a private chat on Rocket Chat alongside the other people of your group. From there you can call the group using the call button on the top of the window!

Discussion skeleton

While you are entirerly free to discuss anything, we provide a few topics which can serve as social facilitator, replacing the usual beer.

  • Who are you?
  • Your research topics
  • The EGSR/MAM talk you preferred so far.
  • What did you learn?
  • What did not understand?
  • What do you look forward to see?
  • Group's research topic
  • Research organization of the Lab/University/Company you work for.

The calls start without your names configured so please update it in the call parameters.
