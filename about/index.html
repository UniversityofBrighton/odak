This is

Eurographics Symposium on Rendering

The 31st edition of Eurographics Symposium on Rendering will be held virtually from June 30th to July 3rd, 2020. EGSR 2020 is being jointly organized by the Realistic Graphics and Imaging group at Imperial College and the Virtual Environments and Computer Graphics group at University College London (UCL). EGSR will be preceded by the 8th annual Workshop on Material Appearance Modeling (MAM) on June 29th, which will also be held virtually.
